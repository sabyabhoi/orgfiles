#+title: Asymptotic Complexity
#+filetags: :CS:

Given two functions ~f(n)~ and ~g(n)~, we say ~g(n)~ is an asymptotic upper bound of ~f(n)~ if \exist a constant ~c~ such that

\[
f(n) \le c \cdot g(n) \quad \forall \; n \ge n_0
\]
