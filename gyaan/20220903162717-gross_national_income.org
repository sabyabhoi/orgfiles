#+title: Gross National Income
#+filetags: :ECONOMICS:

Gross National Income(GNI) is given as the total value of all the goods and services *claimed* by the residents of an economy. In order to get GNI from GDP, we just add the Net Factor incomes from Abroad (NFIA) to the GDP.

\[
GNI = GDP + NFIA
\]
