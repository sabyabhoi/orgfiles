#+title: Asset Management Company
#+filetags: :FINANCE:

- An Asset Management Company(AMC) is established to manage the money of a [[id:0635b621-f216-45ff-91f0-1d8d03ce0628][Mutual Fund]] trust.
- Their main source of income is the fee/commission retrieved by managing the assets.
