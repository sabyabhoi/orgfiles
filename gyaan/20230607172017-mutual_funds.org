#+title: Mutual Funds
#+filetags: :FINANCE:

A mutual fund is a trust, in which people invest their money, and the trustees hire a third party company called an [[id:a313e0ae-9889-4c12-87b3-8a8169a6b609][Asset Management Company]], for managing these assets and buying stocks using the same.
